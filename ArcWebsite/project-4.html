Featured Project

The Gellary

This project transforms a shipping container into a compact yet expressive art gallery. Designed as a flexible exhibition space, the interior maximizes spatial efficiency while maintaining a clean and contemporary aesthetic. Custom display walls, focused lighting, and minimal finishes create an immersive viewing experience. The result is a creative, modern gallery space that proves impactful design can exist within compact structures.
