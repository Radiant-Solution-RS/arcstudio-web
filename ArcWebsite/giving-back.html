GIVING BACK

Investing in People,
Not Just Projects

At RS ARC Studio, we believe strong design begins with strong creative communities. Alongside delivering professional architectural and interior solutions for our clients, we invest time in supporting emerging designers and sharing industry knowledge. By mentoring young talent, participating in design competitions, and developing concept-driven projects, we contribute to the growth of fresh ideas and responsible design practices. This continuous exchange of learning and creativity keeps our studio evolving — and ultimately benefits our clients through innovative thinking and informed design solutions. Because when we grow the creative community, we elevate the quality of design we deliver.

01

Children's Cancer Institute

We've been working with Children's Cancer Institute for a number of years and we're proud to be corporate ambassadors. We've committed to raising over $250,000 through multiple initiatives where we donate for every project we book and install.

02

Workplace Giving

Through our workplace giving initiative, we provide our employees the opportunity to donate to the Children's Cancer Institute on an ongoing basis. BOWERBIRD dollar-match every donation, doubling the efforts of every individual contribution.

03

ReLove Community Support

As part of our commitment to the local community, we've donated trucks full of quality furniture to ReLove. They provide safe homes for women and children impacted by domestic violence by sourcing furniture from corporate partners.

04

Industry Placement Program

We are proud to work with the International School of Colour + Design (iscd), offering an industry-leading Placement Program. We provide first-hand experience to young talent and graduates to bridge the gap between learning and practice.
